What is Robust Spirit ?

Robust Spirit Soulfist is one of the strongest burst damage dealers in the game. Instead of moving from Hype 1 to Hype 3, you could jump in Hype 3 rightaway, which give you tons of damage buff. Also with the skillset being the combination of melee and ranged skill, she make you feel like a pro-gamer playing Lost Ark.

Robust Spirit Soulfist have World Domination as one of the strongest awakening skill and even stronger with RS. With that in mind, missing WD will lead to a huge damage lost and maybe your teammates laughing at you.

Stats Priority

Specialization! This is your main stats with RS Soulfist. Your DMG, ATKSPD, CDR that you get from Hype and your Awakening scale with your Spec. The higher your Spec, the higher your DMG, ATKSPD, CDR your Hype give you and the bigger the Awakening DMG.

Engravings

Engravings are powerfull feature that decide your playstyle in the game. Below is the list of recommended Engravings for playing.

Required meaning it is strongly recommended.

Optional meaning you must pick one engraving within the priority.

Priority meaning in which order that you should get.

Robust Spirit

RequiredPrority: 1

Since this is a Robust Spirit guide, therefore it is a must have Engraving with top priority. Also, you must get LV.3 Robust Spirit since you going use your skill in Hype.

Grudge

RequiredPrority: 2

Grudge is one of the TOP TIER Engraving for every damage dealer in Lost Ark. 20% multiplier damage in exchange for 20% incoming damage. But since this is a dodging game, this seem reasonable.

Precise Dagger

OptionalPrority: 3

Precise Dagger give you flat 20% critical rate in exchange of 10% critical damage which help you in start out spamming rotation without thinking, it is recommended if you don't want to manage too much buff.

Adrenaline

OptionalPrority: 3

Adrenaline provide 15% critical rate plus 6% additional attack power. It is confirm that this give you more DPS than Precise Dagger. But, in exchange, you must maintaining or getting 6 stacks before spamming out rotation.

Keen Blunt Weapon

RequiredPrority: 4

This engraving only bring benefit after you got at least 50-60% critical rate. Therefore I put it in Prority: 4. So you would like this engraving after having enough critical rate, which give you 50% more critical damage.

Cursed Doll

OptionalPrority: 5

This give you 16% additional attack power in exchange of 25% healing reduced. Which is the best engraving within Prority: 5 since every raid will need 1 support in the team.

Mass Increase

OptionalPrority: 5

This is an alternative to Cursed Doll with 10% Attack Speed Reduced. But it is only recommended if you have over 140% Attack Speed.

Awakening

OptionalPrority: 5

If you are a fan of throwing in balls into the boss face, then this is the engraving you need. This will help you throwing out Awakening every 100 seconds.
